Insights...

  • Austin is undoubtedly the most dangerous community in the city having the most number of crimes every year.
  • Even though there are three police districts covering the Austin community, the crime rate is not in check.
  • It has been seen that the criminals take a liking to the first day of every month, and especially the first of January.
  • Special days do affect the crime incidents. For example, on Christmas and Thanksgiving Day, less number of crimes are reported, whereas on the New Year's Day, a significantly higher number of crimes take place.
  • Even though the arrest rate has not improved in the recent years, the crime rate has gone down.
  • For prostitution, the arrest rate is significatly high compared to the other crimes, and many incidents of sexual abuse are reported on the New Year's Day which may be attributed to the large number of parties.
  • Surprisingly, unemployment doesn't affect the crimes in a community to a major extent.
